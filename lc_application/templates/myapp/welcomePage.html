Understanding Letter of Credit

  • A letter of credit is a document from a bank guaranteeing that a seller will receive payment in full as long as certain delivery conditions have been met.
  • If the buyer is unable to make payment on the purchase, the bank will cover the remaining amount.
  • Letters of credit are often used in international transactions to ensure that payment will be received.
